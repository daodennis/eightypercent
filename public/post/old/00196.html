Great BBQ, Scoble!

Sun, Aug 8, 2004

Yesterday Rachel and I went over to the Scoble estate for a BBQ.  It was great to see Robert and Maryam.  The food and company was excellent.  The conversation drifted between geeky things (cameras, HDTV, Tivo/Media Center) and other non geeky things (jokes, BBQ technique, etc.)  I also finially met Mr. Winer -- an interesting experience.
